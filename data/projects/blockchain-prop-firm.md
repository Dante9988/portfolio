# Blockchain Prop Firm 3.0 – Decentralized Trading Challenge Platform

## Overview

A decentralized version of proprietary trading firm infrastructure built on blockchain. Traders participate in on-chain trading challenges where performance metrics are transparently tracked and verified. Successful traders receive smart contract-managed funding allocations with automated profit-sharing mechanics.

## What I Built

### Smart Contract Infrastructure
- Challenge management contracts — creating, tracking, and evaluating trading challenges
- Performance metric verification on-chain (PnL, drawdown, risk limits)
- Funding allocation contracts with automated capital distribution to qualified traders
- Profit-sharing mechanics enforced through smart contract logic

### Trading Platform
- Full-stack application for traders to register, participate in challenges, and track performance
- Real-time trading dashboard with portfolio metrics and challenge progress
- Integration with on-chain data for transparent performance tracking

## Key Highlights
- Trustless trading evaluation — performance verified on-chain, not by a centralized entity
- Smart contract-managed capital allocation removing counterparty risk
- Automated profit-sharing through transparent contract mechanics
- Full-stack TypeScript application with blockchain backend
