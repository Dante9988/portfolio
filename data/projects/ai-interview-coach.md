# AI Interview Coach – Technical Interview Simulator

## Overview

An AI-powered interview coaching platform that simulates realistic technical interviews using LLMs. The system generates interview questions, evaluates responses in real-time, and provides structured feedback on communication clarity, technical accuracy, and problem-solving approach.

**Live demo:** [https://codepilotai.dev/](https://codepilotai.dev/)

## What I Built

### Interview Simulation Engine
- LLM-powered interview question generation tailored to role and difficulty level
- Real-time answer evaluation with scoring across multiple dimensions (technical depth, communication, problem-solving approach)
- Structured feedback generation with specific improvement suggestions
- Multi-round interview flow simulating realistic interview progression

### Frontend (Next.js / TypeScript)
- Modern interview interface with real-time AI feedback
- Session history and progress tracking
- Responsive design for desktop and mobile practice

## Key Highlights
- Realistic interview simulation powered by OpenAI LLMs
- Multi-dimensional answer evaluation (not just correctness)
- Structured feedback helping engineers improve interview performance
- Built with Next.js and TypeScript for a fast, modern UX
